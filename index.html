About

Mie Ayam are a popular Indonesian culinary specialty, loved by many. This dish consists of soft yellow noodles, served with a sweet and savory chicken topping and a rich, warm broth. Besides chicken, green mustard greens, fried onions, and a sprinkling of fresh spring onions are usually added for added flavor. In various regions, Mie Ayam are available in different variations, such as meatballs, boiled dumplings, and even chicken feet. Its deliciousness lies in the distinctive spices that perfectly penetrate the chicken pieces, providing a unique taste sensation. Mie Ayam can be found in everything from simple stalls to large restaurants, at prices that remain affordable. This makes Mie Ayam a favorite everyday menu for many people. Not only is it filling, but Mie Ayam also provide a warm and comforting taste with every mouthful. Its presence always brings nostalgia and togetherness to every meal. It's no wonder Mie Ayam are considered one of Indonesia's timeless culinary icons.
